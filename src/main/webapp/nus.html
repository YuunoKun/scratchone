    National University Of Singapore

                    National University of Singapore is ranked consistently as one of the world's top universities. It has 13 undergraduate schools with 59 bachelor's degree including sciences, medicine and dentistry, design and environment, law, arts and social sciences, engineering, business, computing and music. NUS's main campus is located in the southwestern part of Singapore, adjacent to Kent Ridge. Its Bukit Timah campus houses the Faculty of Law and Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy. The Yale-NUS College is a liberal arts college established in collaboration with Yale University, is located at University Town.
